## Zoom zoom!

The modern age is hyper-digital, now more than ever. As an Agency that specializes in digital content, we needed an upgrade to make our upload and downloading process ultra-efficient.  
  
We decided to have [Everstream](https://everstream.net/) install fiber at the studio to take our current upload (30mb/s) and download (400mb/s) speed to 950 megabits per second for both our downloads and uploads.  
  
We knew this upgrade would improve our workflow, but after spinning these blazing speeds for a week, we can’t imagine how we existed without Fiber in the past. GodSPEED everyone.
