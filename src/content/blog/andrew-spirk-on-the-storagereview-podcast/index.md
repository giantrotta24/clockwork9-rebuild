## TrueNAS Means Speed for Creative Agencies

Our Managing & Creative Director Andrew Spirk was featured on the [StorageReview Podcast](https://www.storagereview.com/podcast/podcast-74-truenas-means-speed-for-creative-agencies) with [Brian Beeler](https://www.linkedin.com/in/brian-beeler-7831b91/).

In the episode, they discuss our evolution from an off-the-shelf NAS to the all-flash [TrueNAS M40](https://www.truenas.com/m-series/) storage platform and [TrueNas Mini XL+](https://www.truenas.com/truenas-mini/) from [iXSystems](https://www.ixsystems.com). They also discuss the challenges creative agencies face, our workload profile, and how our old solution failed to deliver and the TrueNAS systems saved the day.
