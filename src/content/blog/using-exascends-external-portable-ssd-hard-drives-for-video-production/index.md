There’s no room for compromise when capturing footage for our latest video project. Time is precious, and every detail matters. For those pushing the limits of data transfer, reliable, rugged, and high-speed storage isn’t just a luxury—it’s essential. That’s where [Exascend’s External Portable SSD Hard Drives](https://cinema.exascend.com/product-overview/?filters=product_cat%5B65%5D) excel.

It’s been a while since our team had portable storage solutions advanced enough to meet the diverse needs of our video production agency. After testing them in the studio and on set, we found that the [Exascend](https://exascend.com/) portable drive lineup exceeded our expectations.

## Element: Portable Power for Creators on the Move

Shooting reels and social content is part of our daily workflow at C9, and when we’re working with high-res files on [the latest iPhone](https://www.apple.com/iphone/), storage fills up fast. The [Exascend Element Portable SSD](https://exascend.com/element-portable-ssd/) solves that problem effortlessly. Compact, versatile, and with a magnetic feature to keep everything organized, it’s the ideal solution for fast file transfers to our editing machines. At just 2.75” x 1.75” x 0.3125”, it’s one of the smallest SSDs we’ve used.

Key features like sustained 2000MB/s read/write speeds, the ability to record directly from an iPhone at up to 4K 60FPS in ProRes, OTG support for Android, a 20Gbps USB-C port, and a 5-year warranty make the Element SSD a go-to for creators who need performance in a compact package.

## Gecko: Built for Adventure, Ready for Anything

Our team is always in the field, hiking, fishing, or filming wildlife. Instead of juggling memory cards in less-than-ideal conditions, we rely on [Exascend’s Gecko](https://cinema.exascend.com/products/portable-ssd-category/gecko-portable-ssd/) to keep our media safe. With its IP67 rating, this drive is designed to withstand water, dirt, heat, and extreme cold, offering peace of mind in any environment.

One of the standout features of the Gecko is its quick plate system. We can connect it directly to our camera, mount it on a gimbal, and confidently shoot all day. Other notable features include 1,700MB/s read/write speeds, USB-C 20Gbps capabilities, and [Exascend’s standard 5-year warranty](https://exascend.com/support/warranty/).

## Nitro: Unmatched Speed for High-End Cinematography

Speed and reliability are crucial on professional sets, and the [Exascend Nitro](https://cinema.exascend.com/products/portable-ssd-category/nitro-portable-ssd/) delivers on both fronts. With a cutting-edge USB4 interface, 40Gbps transfer speeds, and an IP68 dust and waterproof rating, the Nitro handles real-time color grading, playback, and on-set editing without breaking a sweat.

It’s ideal for demanding tasks and has the same magnetic feature I love in the Element. Additional highlights include 3,100MB/s read speeds, 3,000MB/s write speeds, a USB-C connection, and a 40Gbps USB-C cable, all backed by a 5-year warranty. For those who require peak performance in portable storage, the Nitro is the top choice.

## Real-World Performance and Considerations

As with any SSD, expect a slight difference between the advertised speeds and real-world performance. This is standard across the industry, and Exascend’s drives offer impressive speeds for both consumer and professional markets.

One issue we noticed with the Nitro was its lack of compatibility with [Thunderbolt 5 cables](https://geni.us/thunderbolt5), which didn’t provide any performance advantage. However, all other 40Gbps cables worked flawlessly at maximum speed. We also use [Hedge software](https://hedge.co/) to transfer files to [our NAS servers](https://www.postmagazine.com/Publications/Post-Magazine/2021/March-April-2021/Open-Storage-sees-gains-in-post-production.aspx), and while our server setup can bottleneck transfer speeds, the Nitro still performs admirably under typical conditions.

## The Future of Storage is Here

The right tools can make the difference between a smooth and stressful project. Exascend’s portable SSDs have become indispensable to our workflow, ensuring that every shot and frame is safely preserved, no matter where we are.

While these SSDs cater to different needs, they all share a common goal—keeping your files secure. Whether you’re a casual creator or a seasoned professional, choosing the drive that fits your requirements is essential. Working with high-end cinema cameras demands reliable storage solutions at every stage. Exascend delivers on that promise with rugged reliability, lightning-fast speeds, and the assurance that our vision is in good hands.
